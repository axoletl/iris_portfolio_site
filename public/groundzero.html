We live in a time of tension. The discussions of nuclear threat have become ever normalised and common in our daily lives. In Ground 0, I explored the stories, opinions and experiences of humanity tending towards nuclear conflict. In doing so, I aim to direct readers towards direct action against nuclear threat.

Read the full publication on Issuu!

Made with Adobe Indesign, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

Additional mockups created in Blender.

Throughout the publication, chapters mark a rating on the DEFCON system, populated by the stories associated with that rating. Lower ratings are closer to complete nuclear conflict, and therefore are filled with eyewitness accounts and stories of the effects of nuclear war.

Data visualisations bookend chapters to give real world context and scale to the subjective stories shared.
